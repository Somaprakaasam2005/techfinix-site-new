About Techfinix'25

Techfinix'25 is an annual college symposium organized by the Department of Information Technology at Paavai Engineering College. It's a platform for students to showcase innovation, attend workshops, participate in competitions, and network with industry experts.

Theme: Emerging Technologies & Responsible Innovation. Join us for two days of learning, challenge, and inspiration.
